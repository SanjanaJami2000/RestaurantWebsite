Click on Order Online. Select your items and go to the cart. Pick a delivery option and a payment option. Give any additional information required and proceed to pay. You will get an order confirmation and number which you may track using the tracking link.


You may Pay online or at the store. You will be redirected to a payment gateway, make your payment and you will get an order confirmation. When paying at the store, you will get a confirmation, pay when you pick the order up.


Order fulfilments options can be seen on the cart page once you select your items.
